The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design,dense plantings, and a mixture of ormamental and edible plants

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in1870's England,when stylized versions were formed as a
reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained Englich estate ardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants,with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs,along with some fruit trees.
