Briards

a heart wrapped in fur

The briads,or berger de brie, is a large of dog traditionaly used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briad, which is belived to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep.The breed was used by the
French Army as sentries,messengers and to search for wounded soldiers beacause of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the First
World War almost to the point of extinction.Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering.Charlemagne,Napoleon,Thomas
Jefferson and Lafayette all owened briards.
